Professional portrait

Tachary O’Neill is the founder of Trustworthy Solutions, built on the belief that access to financial clarity and strategic planning should not be reserved for a select few. Raised in a household where finances were not openly discussed, she witnessed firsthand how a lack of preparation can amplify life’s most difficult transitions—particularly during times of loss.

Those experiences shaped her mission: to help individuals, families, and businesses create structure, foresight, and resilience through informed financial decisions. Through O’Neill Business Solutions Team, Tachary bridges education and execution—connecting clients with tools, resources, and professional networks that support stability, growth, and long-term confidence.
